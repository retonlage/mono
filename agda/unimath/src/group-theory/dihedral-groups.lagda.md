## Idea

The dihedral group `D_k` is defined by the dihedral group construction applied
to the cyclic group `ℤ-Mod k`.

## Definition

```agda
dihedral-group : ℕ → Group lzero
dihedral-group k = dihedral-group-Ab (ℤ-Mod-Ab k)
```
